Stay Sharp: Scam Awareness Quiz

This quiz is designed to encourage older adults to stay alert online. It focuses on real situations such as scam calls, fake urgency, money requests, suspicious links, and AI-generated images.

This is not a test to judge anyone. Many scams are designed to feel emotional, urgent, and believable. The goal is to practice noticing warning signs and making safer choices.

Reminder

  • Scammers often use urgency, fear, or family emotion.
  • It is always okay to pause and verify first.
  • Do not trust a message just because it sounds personal or official.
  • AI images and fake identities can look very real.
